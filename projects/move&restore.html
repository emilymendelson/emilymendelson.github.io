Move & Restore Website

  • Move & Restore is a web platform allowing for tradition physiotherapy clinics to easily move online.

    The platform provides a web repository of easy to follow exercise videos, which allows physiotherapists to customize a systematic exercise plan for their patients. To promote communication between physiotherapists and patients, we also provide a journal and a live chat feature to let patients keep track of their progress and share any feedback directly with their physiotherapist.

    To ensure the confidentiality of patient information we are looking to implement various measures including, but not limited to: installing a Secure Sockets Layer, anti-malware software, running regular backups, and using an industry standard encryption algorithm.

    Utilized: PHP, MySQL Databases, LAMP Stack, CSS, HTML
